Title I Content

Treadwell International Community School is a schoolwide Title I school. We partner with families through our Professional Learning Community (PLC) to raise achievement for every student.

As a Title I school, Treadwell International Community School receives federal funding to strengthen teaching and learning — and the law guarantees families a voice in how that happens. Your involvement matters. Below you'll find our annual meeting, key documents, and the people who can help you get connected.
